  1. Count the positive integers that divide at least one of these numbers: \(10^{100}, 20^{50}, 30^{40}\).

  2. Let \(X:=\{0,1,\ldots, k\}\) and \(f : X^n \to \mathbb{Z}\) denote the function \(f(x_1, x_2, \ldots, x_n)\). Find \(|f^{-1}(m)|\) for some \(m\in \mathbb{Z}\).

  3. Given a finite set \(X\) and a subset \(A\), count the number of subsets of \(X\) of cardinality \(k\) that are supersets of \(A\).

  4. From chapter 4 of the textbook: exercises 8, 11, 19, 24
