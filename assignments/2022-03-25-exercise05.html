  1. Prove that each vertex of a graph belongs to exactly one component.

  2. Prove that every tree, \(T\), has at least \(\Delta(T)\) leaves.

  3. Given a tree, \(T\), let \(f : V(T) \to V(T)\) be a bijection so that \(\{x,y\} \in E(T) \iff \{f(x),f(y)\} \in E(T)\). Prove that \(f\) either fixes at least one vertex or fixes an edge.

  4. Prove that every connected graph contains a path of length at least \(\textrm{min} \{2\delta(G), |G| - 1\}\).

  5. Prove that every 2-connected graph contains a cycle.
